Coronavirus COVID-19

Together, we’ll get coronavirus under control in the Netherlands. On this website you can find information about what the Dutch government – together with other organisations – is doing.

Help prevent the spread of coronavirus

Stay home as much as possible. Work from home if you can. You should only go outside to shop for essentials (do this alone), to get some fresh air or to care for another person. Always stay 1.5 metres from other people (2 arm's length). Wash your hands often with soap and water for 20 second, then dry them thoroughly.

Advice and information on the new coronavirus

The National Institute for Public Health and the Environment (RIVM) is providing advice and information on the new coronavirus. The organisation provides advice to doctors, laboratories and municipal health services (GGDs) on tackling the virus in the Netherlands. You can find detailed information on the new coronavirus on the RIVM website.
