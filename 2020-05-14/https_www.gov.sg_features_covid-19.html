Updates on the COVID-19 situation in Singapore

Latest update

As of 13 May 2020, 12pm, MOH has confirmed an additional 675 cases of COVID-19 infection in Singapore.

Separately, 958 more cases of COVID-19 infection have been discharged from hospitals or community isolation facilities.

More info on the cases

For latest updates, go to www.moh.gov.sg
