National Coronavirus telephone line in several languages

Call 08-123 680 00 for answers to questions about COVID-19.

The line is open on weekdays between 09:00 and 15:00. If you call the line outside of these hours, you can leave a message and be called back.

The telephone line is staffed by health communication officers who speak English, Arabic, Somali, Farsi, Dari, Tigrinya, Amharic, Russian, Bosnian, Croatian, Serbian, and Spanish.

If you or anyone close to you is sick and you have any questions about their illness, always call 1177. In the event of an emergency and life-threatening difficulties, always call 112.

The telephone line is available in several languages and is a collaboration between the Public Health Agency of Sweden and local regions.

About the website

The Public Health Agency of Sweden is an expert authority with responsibility for public health issues at a national level. The Agency develops and supports activities to promote health, prevent illness and improve preparedness for health threats. Our vision statement: a public health that strengthens the positive development of society.
