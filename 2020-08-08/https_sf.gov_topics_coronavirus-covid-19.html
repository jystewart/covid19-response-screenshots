Coronavirus (COVID-19)

Stay home as much as possible. Avoid gatherings. Wear face coverings. Get help in the coronavirus pandemic.

Wear a face covering

You must wear something to cover your mouth and nose when you are within 6 feet of people who don't live with you.

Coronavirus can be spread before you feel sick, when you breathe, talk, cough, or sneeze. If we all wear face coverings, we help prevent the spread of COVID-19, and save lives.
