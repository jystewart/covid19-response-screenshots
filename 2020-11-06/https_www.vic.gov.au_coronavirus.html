Latest advice

From 11:59pm Tuesday 27 October 2020 some restrictions in metropolitan Melbourne and regional Victoria are easing. See the roadmap for reopening page for more information.

Northern metro region community outbreak: Any residents of Melbourne’s northern suburbs including Dallas, Roxburgh Park, Broadmeadows, Preston and West Heidelberg who are experiencing symptoms are being urged to be tested for coronavirus. For more information, visit the Department of Health and Human Services.
